⚠ Important Disclaimer
This tool provides diagnostic guidance and control recommendations based on psychrometric calculations. It is intended as a decision-support aid for trained HVAC/BMS engineers.

All control actions must be verified and approved by the site engineer or facility manager before implementation. Do not apply control changes to live systems — especially operating theatres or critical care areas — without authorised sign-off.

Calculations assume standard atmospheric pressure (101.325 kPa). Results may vary under different site conditions. Smart Joules and the tool authors accept no liability for decisions made solely on the basis of this tool's output.
